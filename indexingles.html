ABOUT US


Who are we?

We are a non-profit organization whose purpose to show to the society the love and commitment with people living in vulnerability, teaching that with a change of mentality and lifestyle you can modify our future and the future of our environment. Created in 2010, Salt of the world is a foundation initially formed as a ministry of support to the most vulnerable sectors in the city of Bogota, but soon became the foundation evangelist who is today.

What do we do?

Draft CHRISTIAN WORLD program oriented to the improvement of the quality of life of children and young people of the neighborhood Ciudad Bolivar and their families, through pedagogical strategies guided by the playful, recreation, the game and the arts. Based on the foregoing, it was also thought in the development of young people and the implementation of a plan of technical improvement work which prevents the young population have problems with gangs and drugs. How do we do it?

Volunteering. To become volunteer you must have: * A firm commitment to the values and principles of volunteerism. * Ability to work in a multicultural environment. * Ability to adapt to difficult living conditions and especially passion for service. You can join us in a brigade and visit the Foundation; we receive donations: school, musical instruments, books, clothes, food, medicines or carry donations in money for supplies or even linked to our program of sponsoring a child.

You can help us

DONATIONS help us to create possibilities in places where there are only dust and unknowns dreams. Donations in Colombia through the savings account of Banco Colpatria number 462-200-404-5 Foundation in the name of "Salt of the world". You can also make a donation in kind, we need: clothing, toys, school supplies, books of primary and secondary school, non-perishable food, crayon (temperas), bookmarks, musical instruments, materials for handicrafts, among others.
